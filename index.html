Emergency Support

How It Works?

Step 1: Enter Symptoms:

Describe the health issue

Step 2: The AI will classify the problem into 3 cases based on its severity.

Case 1:

If the health problem is mild then it will give a quick AI diagnosis which will include immediate AI driven medical advice and dietary advice which can solve the problem.

Case 2:

If the health problem is a bit bad and cannot be solved without proper medical advice by a doctor, then it will recommend nearby doctors along with their patient ratings as well as their contact number and an option to book an appointment.

Case 3:

If the health problem is very severe then it will recommend the nearest hospitals as well as an option to book an ambulance so that immediate action can be taken.
